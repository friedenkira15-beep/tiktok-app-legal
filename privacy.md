# Privacy Policy

**VideoClipper**

*Last updated: December 11, 2025*

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how VideoClipper ("we", "our", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our video processing and TikTok integration service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- TikTok account information (via OAuth authentication)
- Video content you choose to process and upload

### 2.2 Information from TikTok
When you connect your TikTok account, we may receive:
- Your TikTok username and display name
- Your TikTok user ID
- Basic profile information

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information
- Log data (IP address, browser type, access times)
- Usage information (features used, interactions)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide the video processing and upload service
- Authenticate your TikTok account
- Upload content to TikTok on your behalf (only with your explicit consent)
- Improve and maintain the Service
- Respond to your requests or questions

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:
- With TikTok, as necessary to provide the upload functionality
- If required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or the safety of users

## 5. TikTok API Usage

Our Service uses TikTok's Content Posting API. When you connect your account:
- We only request permissions necessary for the Service to function
- We only upload content when you explicitly initiate the action
- You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time

## 6. Data Retention

- Video content is processed temporarily and not stored permanently on our servers
- TikTok authentication tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time
- We retain minimal data necessary to provide the Service

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Encrypted storage of authentication tokens
- Limited access to personal data

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Disconnect your TikTok account
- Opt out of the Service at any time

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 10. International Users

If you are accessing the Service from the European Union or other regions with data protection laws, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## 12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
